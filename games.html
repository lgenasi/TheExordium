News:

    -- 06 July 2015 --

    New FTB Minecraft Server

    We have started up a new Minecraft Server using the FTB modpack "Infinity". You can see more details and a live view of the map here: Minecraft Map


    Gilly has also started work on our own modpack that will contain many of our favourite mods while keeping the server as stable as possible. So far he has over 200 mods! Once the pack is ready we will be using the 'SKCraft Launcher' to deploy and keep the mods updated. More details to come when the pack has finished being tested!

    In April a group of the Exordium Community met-up to play a variety of games all under one roof, LAN style. We also went paint-balling to stretch the legs a bit. A decision most regretted later.
